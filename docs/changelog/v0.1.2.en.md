[Shinobu Miyahara](https://www.linkedin.com/in/miyashino/) has joined Unbounded Pioneering Inc. as an outside advisor. At [Recruit](https://www.recruit.co.jp/), [Plaid](https://plaid.co.jp/), and [IVRy](https://ivry.jp/), he has consistently driven new business launches through to growth. Across both listed companies and startups, he has led the design, implementation, and scaling of business structures in the enterprise space, spanning business development (BizDev), product management, and alliances. Drawing on this experience, he will drive the strengthening of the business strategy and organizational structure of "Turnint AI", an Agentic AI platform that accelerates business growth across sales and HR areas.
